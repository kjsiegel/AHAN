Asians for Humans and Nature (AHAN)

Asians for Humans, Animals and Nature is a non-profit organization dedicated to educating the public on animal and environmental welfare

About

We hope by reading our educational material, people can have a better understanding of how humans and animals are all part of this world, when animals become extinct, human extinction won’t be too far along. We hope our material can open people’s heart and learn how to respect all animals and nature that we share on this earth. They too can become an important part of our expansion and teach their families and friends what they learn..


Mission and Goals

Reduce Animal Suffering To find homes for every adoptable animal we take in. Whenever possible, help other organizations or rescuers with their adoption programs by providing foster care and posting available animals on our website
